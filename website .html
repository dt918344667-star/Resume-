Green Mobility for a Cleaner Future

Introduction to Green Mobility

Green mobility focuses on sustainable and eco-friendly transportation methods such as electric vehicles, public transport, cycling, and walking. By reducing dependence on fossil fuels, we can minimize pollution and contribute to cleaner air in cities like Delhi.

Pollution Awareness

Air pollution in Delhi is a growing concern. Green mobility solutions can significantly reduce harmful emissions and help create a healthier, greener future for everyone.

Environmental Issues & Delhi’s Air Pollution

Why Delhi’s Pollution Is a Concern

Delhi frequently records extremely high Air Quality Index (AQI) levels. Rapid urbanization, heavy vehicle traffic, industrial emissions, and construction dust contribute to severe air pollution, affecting public health.

Main Causes

  • Vehicular emissions
  • Factory smoke
  • Construction dust
  • Stubble burning in nearby states
  • Weather conditions trapping pollutants

How Green Mobility Helps

Green mobility promotes electric vehicles, cycling, metro, walking, and other clean transport modes. These solutions reduce emissions and support healthier, sustainable urban living.
